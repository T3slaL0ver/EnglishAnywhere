示例對話 Example Conversations


Q: 你的愛好是什麼？？What are your hobbies?

A: 我喜歡烹飪和編織。I like to cook and knit.


Q: 你沒事時喜歡做什麼？What do you do for fun?

A: 我喜歡去登山。I like to go hiking.


Q: 無聊的時候你會做什麼？What do you do when you're bored?

A: 我喜歡畫畫、閱讀和寫作。I like to draw, read, and write.


Q: 你在家有什麼喜歡做的事？What do you like to do at home?

A: 我喜歡畫畫、閱讀和寫作。I like to play video games.
