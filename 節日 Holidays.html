例句 Example Sentences


Q: 你最喜歡的節日是什麼？What’s your favorite holiday?

Q: 你們家感恩節時會做什麼？What do you and your family do during Thanksgiving?

A: 我們會吃火雞。We eat turkey.


Q: 你會和誰一起過年？Who will you be spending New Year’s Eve with?

A: 我會和家人一起過年。I will celebrate New Year's Eve with my family.


Q: 今年暑假你要做什麼？What are you doing over the summer?

A: 我要去度假。I am going on vacation.
