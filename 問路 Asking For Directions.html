示例對話 Example Conversations


Q: 公園在哪裡？Where is the park?

A: 公園離超級市場很近。The park is near the supermarket.


Q: 醫院離這裡多遠？How far is the hospital from here?

A: 醫院離這裡很遠。The hospital is very far from here.


Q: 醫院離這裡多遠？How do I get to the restaurant?

A: 直走再右轉你就會看到餐廳。Go straight ahead, make a right, and you will see the restaurant.
