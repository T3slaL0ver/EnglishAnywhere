Asking For Directions 问路

Basic Sentences 基本句型


____ 在哪里? - Where is ____?


____ 多远？ - How far is ____？


我怎么去（那里） - How do I get there？





Navigating 导航


方向 - Direction


地方 - Place


左边 - Left


在____的左边 - To the left of (the) ____


右边 - Right


在____的右边 - To the right of (the) ____


前方 - Straight Ahead


进 - near


远 - far

医院 - Hospital



Example Conversations 示例对话


Q: Where is the park? 公园在哪里？

A: The park is near the supermarket. 公园离超级市场很近。


Q: How far is the hospital from here? 医院离这里多远？

A: The hospital is very far from here. 医院离这里很远。


Q: How do I get to the restaurant? 医院离这里多远？

A: Go straight ahead, make a right, and you will see the restaurant. 直走再右转你就会看到餐厅。
