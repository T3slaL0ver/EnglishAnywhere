例句 Example Sentences


Q: 您今天飛往哪裡？Where are you flying to today?

Q: 您幾點登機? What is your boarding time?

A: 我的登機時間是晚上七點。My boarding time is 7 pm.


Q: 你今天搭乘哪家航空公司的航班？What airline are you flying with today?

A: 我搭乘全日空的航班。I am flying with ANA.
