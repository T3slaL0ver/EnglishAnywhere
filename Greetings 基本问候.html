你好嗎/您好嗎？- How are you? 🤗

昨天 - Yesterday 📅



後天 - The Day After Tomorrow 📅

星期二 - Tuesday 📅

星期四 - Thursday 📅

Example Conversations 示例對話


Q: Hi! How are you? 你好嗎？

A: I'm great, thank you! 我很好，謝謝！


Q: What day of the week is it today? 今天是星期幾？

A: Today is Sunday. 今天是星期天。
